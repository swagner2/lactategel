Why lactate
A fuel, a shuttle, and a signal.

Lactate isn't a waste product — it's central to how endurance athletes make and move energy. LactateGel is built to harness three of its roles at once.

01 / FUEL

Ready-to-burn energy

Lactate is a rapidly oxidised fuel the body reaches for readily — potentially taking a different absorption route than glucose and fructose.

02 / SPARE

Protect your glycogen

At steady efforts, added lactate may help shift the fuel mix and spare precious glycogen for when the race really demands it.

03 / SIGNAL

A metabolic messenger

Beyond fuel, lactate signals the cell how to use other substrates — a role increasingly recognised in exercise physiology.
